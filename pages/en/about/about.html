Sustainability

We think about the future, we act now .

Cavaliere Cargo believes that small actions can improve the world. Therefore, we constantly invest in projects related to sports, health, culture and education, as well as other activities that value the human being and provide economic development with a view to the future of the planet.

In the operational aspect, Cavaliere Cargo invests in sustainable actions to reduce emissions of greenhouse gases as much as possible.

Our initiatives seek to increase the efficiency of assemblies through the use of green fuels and transport with up to 3 temperatures, reduction of trips with empty vehicle, performance improvement aerodynamics of mounts with the installation of baffles and investments in more efficient engines, such as those of the Euro 5 class, less polluting.
