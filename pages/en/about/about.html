Sustainability

We think about the future, we act now .

We believe that small actions
can improve the world.

For this reason we constantly invest in projects related to sports, health, culture and education , as well as other activities that value the human being and provide economic development with a view to the future of the region.

On the operational and logistical side, we invest in sustainable actions to reduce carbon emissions as much as possible.

Our initiatives seek to increase the efficiency of assemblies through the use of green fuels and transport with up to 3 temperatures, reduction of trips with an empty vehicle, improvement of the aerodynamic performance of assemblies with the installation of deflectors and investments in more efficient engines, such as the Euro 5 class, less polluting.
